About Us

Hoshis' Library is a library that focuses on making music more accessible to the community. Music has bonded people across didffertn culutres and parts of the world for centuries and with the interenet, the rapid spread of music seems to go on forever. However, it has become extremely difficult for smaller musicians to break through in todays market with how staturated it has become. Hoshis' Library aims to remedy that by giving local and smaller artists a platform and community to grow from by hosting their works in our database. This gives artists the ability to push their music out to a tangeble audience without having to worry about higher management or sales-pitching to big corporations.
